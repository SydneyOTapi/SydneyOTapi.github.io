Welcome to my portfolio.

I'm a junior at Lehigh University. I'm originally from Maryland, right outside of Washington, D.C. I packed my bags and moved to Bethlehem, PA to study Journalism, Graphic Design, and Global Studies. This semester, I decided to enroll in a Data Visualization course, hoping to combine my love for writing and design in a creative and interdiciplinary structure. I've learned a lot this semester and you can check it out here!

What I've Created

I had the chance to create a variety of content while mixing written word and unique design solutions. I learned to use popular applications that are taking the Data Visualization world by storm.
