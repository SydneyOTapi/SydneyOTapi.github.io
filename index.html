My words, my vision and my art through a global lens

Welcome to my world.

I'm a junior at Lehigh University. I'm originally from Maryland, right outside of Washington, D.C. I packed my bags and moved to Bethlehem, PA to study Journalism, Graphic Design, and Global Studies. This semester, I decided to enroll in a Data Visualization course, hoping to combine my love for writing and design in a creative and interdiciplinary structure. I've learned a lot this semester and you can check it out here!

My work

I had the chance to create a variety of content while mixing written word and unique design solutions. I learned to use popular applications that are taking the Data Visualization world by storm.

    // I first created an infographic using Canva. As a woman at Lehigh I wanted to explore the changing gender percentages. I discovered the class of 2020 is 55 percent male. infographic

    My love for music led me to create a Google fusion map of the concert venues I've been to.

    I am a news editor for Lehigh's biweekly newspaper,The Brown and White. I questioned the history of the publication, so I constructed a timeline with TimelineJS to share its fascinating past.

    As a member of the College of Arts and Sciences, I was interested in whether enrollment has increased or decreased within the last few years. I had then made a chart with Chartbuilder to show enrollment trends from 2006-15.

    Tableau proved to be an extremely helpful tool this past semester. I used the application to make a wide variety of visualizations, including this interactive barchart to show arrests on local college campuses.

    I realized the importance of illustration in communication, and created a comic using Pixton to introduce myself in a creative way.
